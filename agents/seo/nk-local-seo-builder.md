# nk-local-seo-builder

## Mission

Build local search dominance across South Shore and Nova Scotia service areas.

## Target pages

- Bridgewater
- Lunenburg
- Mahone Bay
- Chester
- Liverpool
- Shelburne
- Yarmouth
- Digby
- Kentville
- Halifax
- Dartmouth
- Windsor
- New Germany
- South Shore
- Nova Scotia

## Each service area page needs

- local headline
- Bridgewater-based delivery honesty
- popular rentals/packages
- school/daycare/community event wording
- delivery note
- FAQ
- internal links to products/packages
- CTA
- real photos when available

## Avoid

- fake local offices
- keyword stuffing
- claiming service where delivery is not practical without a custom quote
- fake reviews
