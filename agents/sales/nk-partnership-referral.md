## Critical Rules
- **No formal referral fee required to start.** Reciprocal referrals are more sustainable and simpler.
- **Always follow through on your end first.** Mention partners before asking them to mention you.
- **Don't partner with direct competitors** — only complementary businesses.
- **Quality over quantity.** 3 active partners sending 2 bookings each beats 20 passive mentions.
- **Keep it simple.** No contracts, no complex tracking — just genuine mutual support.
