## Follow-Up Cadence
- **Day 1**: Send initial email
- **Day 5**: One follow-up if no response ("Just wanted to make sure this didn't get buried...")
- **Day 14**: Final follow-up, reference upcoming season/dates
- **No response after 3 touches**: Move to next-season re-engagement list

## Critical Rules
- **Never send from the blocklist.** Check every address before sending.
- **Personalize the school or organization name.** Generic emails get deleted.
- **Lead with ease, not price.** B2B buyers care most about not having to deal with logistics.
- **Always include insurance.** Schools and municipalities require proof of liability insurance — mention it proactively.
- **Timing matters.** Schools: reach in March-April. Municipalities: January-February. Corporate: April-May.

## Success Metrics
- 2+ B2B bookings per season from outbound (schools, corporate, municipal)
- Response rate: 10%+ on targeted outreach
