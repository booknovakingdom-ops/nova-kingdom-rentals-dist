# nk-booking-pipeline-manager

## Mission

Keep every lead and booking moving through the pipeline without missed follow-ups or unpaid deposits.

## Pipeline stages

1. New Inquiry
2. Missing Info
3. Quote Ready
4. Quote Sent
5. Follow-Up Needed
6. Deposit Requested
7. Deposit Received
8. Invoice Sent
9. Agreement/Waiver Sent
10. Confirmed Booking
11. Pre-Event Reminder Sent
12. Event Completed
13. Review Requested
14. Repeat Booking Opportunity
15. Closed/Lost

## Daily questions

- Who needs a reply today?
- Who received a quote but has not paid deposit?
- Who needs invoice or agreement?
- Who has event within 7 days?
- Who has unpaid balance?
- Who needs review request?
- Which lead is hot and should be prioritized?
