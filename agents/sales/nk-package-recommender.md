# Nova Kingdom Package Recommender

## 5 Questions → Recommendation
1. Age of kids? 2. How many guests? 3. Event type? 4. Budget? 5. Want water?

## By Age
Toddlers 2-5 → Crown Dino Combo ($210) or Dino Dash ($310)
Kids 5-10 → Crown Cascade ($260) or Quest & Games ($449)
Mixed 3-12 → Crown Island Combo ($310) or Island Splash ($370)
Older kids + teens → Crown Rush 42 ($450) or Royal All-Star ($849)
Adults + teens → Crown Axe + Crown Kick Darts + lawn games

## By Budget
Under $250 → Crown Dino Combo ($210)
$250-$350 → Crown Quest/Cascade/Climber/Island Combo
$350-$500 → Crown Rush 42 ($450) or Cascade Starter ($320)
$500-$700 → Quest & Games ($449), Dino Party Plus ($549), Island Royale ($649)
$700-$1,000 → Royal All-Star ($849)
$1,000+ → Kingdom Deluxe ($1,099) or Ultimate Kingdom ($1,499)

## Scenarios
Small toddler birthday 8 kids → Crown Dino Combo + Ring Toss
Kids birthday 15 kids summer → Crown Cascade wet + Giant Jenga
Mixed party adults + kids 25 ppl → Crown Quest + Crown Axe + lawn games
Big birthday 30+ kids → Island Royale or Royal All-Star
School field day 80 students → Crown Rush 42 + Crown Axe + full lawn games
Corporate team BBQ 50 adults → Crown Axe + Crown Kick Darts + all 12 lawn games

## Lawn Game Upgrade Logic
Package includes 5 games → "Upgrade to all 12 for just $100 more"
Package has no games → "Add 5 games for $150 or all 12 for $250"
