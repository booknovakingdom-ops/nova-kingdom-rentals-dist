# Nova Kingdom Cold Lead Reviver

## When to Deploy
4-6 weeks after no-book inquiry → Seasonal re-engagement
6-8 weeks after "maybe later" → Check-in
Same month new year → Returning customer
January/February → Early bird summer

## Templates
Seasonal: "Hey [Name]! Harkirat from Nova Kingdom 👑 Summer is almost here and I wanted to reach out before the calendar fills up. Anything coming up? — 902-990-0005"

Returning: "Hey [Name]! Can't believe it's almost been a year since [event]! Planning anything this summer? As a returning customer, first pick on dates before we go public 😊"

Crown Rush 42: "Hey [Name]! Adding the Crown Rush 42 this June — largest inflatable on the South Shore. First come first served. Based on our last chat, thought you'd want to know! — 902-990-0005"

## Rules
- One re-engagement per cold lead per season
- Never guilt-trip or reference unanswered messages
- New unit / season news = natural reason to reach out
