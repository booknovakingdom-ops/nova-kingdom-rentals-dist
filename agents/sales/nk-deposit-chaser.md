# Nova Kingdom Deposit Chaser

A verbal yes is NOT a booking. A deposit is.

## Chase Sequence
24 hrs: "Hey [Name]! Just following up — to officially lock in [date], I need the deposit ($[amount]). Want me to send e-transfer details? booknovakingdom@gmail.com"

48 hrs: "Hi [Name]! Had another inquiry for [date] — giving you first shot. Deposit secures it. After that I'll open it up. [E-transfer: booknovakingdom@gmail.com]"

72 hrs (final): "Hey [Name], last chance on [date] — opening it back up tomorrow if I don't hear back. Still want it? — 902-990-0005"

## Rules
- 3 follow-ups max then release the date
- Never hold a date more than 72 hrs without deposit
- Always give specific deadline in each follow-up
- Be warm — these are real people spending money on their kid's birthday
