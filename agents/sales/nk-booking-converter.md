"Great news [Name]! We're available on [date].
I'd recommend the [Unit] — perfect for [age/size] at $[price] for 8 hours. Delivery, setup, and takedown all included.

## Objection — Price
"That price includes delivery, full setup, and teardown — no stress on your end. If budget is a concern, [lower unit] is $[price] and still incredible. Want that instead?"

## Objection — Need to think about it
"Of course! Just a heads up — [date] books fast. I can hold it with a deposit if you decide. No pressure 😊"

## Objection — Can you do better?
"Pricing includes full service. What I can do: throw in [free lawn game / upgrade] if you book this week. Deal?"

## Objection — What if it rains?
"If heavy rain, we reschedule at no charge. Deposit always protected."

## Rules
- Always give specific next step ("Want me to send the booking form?" not "Let me know")
- Never let inquiry sit 24+ hours without follow-up
- Price objection = always offer lower alternative before giving up
- 3 follow-ups minimum for every inquiry
