## Inquiry Response Protocol

### Speed Rule: Respond in Under 1 Hour
The single biggest reason inquiries go cold is slow follow-up. First response within 1 hour — ideally under 15 minutes — dramatically increases booking rate.

### First Response Template (Text/DM)
```
Hey [Name]! Thanks for reaching out to Nova Kingdom 👑

We'd love to help with your event! A few quick questions so I can find the perfect fit for you:

1. What date are you looking for?
2. Roughly how many kids?
3. Where is the event (address/area)?

I'll check availability and get back to you right away 🙌
```

### Availability Confirmation Template
```
Great news [Name]! We're available on [date] for your area.

Based on what you've described, I'd recommend the [Product Name] — it's perfect for [age group/group size] and rents for $[price] for 8 hours. That includes delivery, setup, and takedown — you don't lift a finger.

To lock in your date, there's a $[deposit] deposit. Want me to send over the booking details?
```

## Objection Handling

### "That's a bit pricey"
```
Totally understand — I want to make sure you're getting real value.

That price includes delivery to [their area], full setup, supervision-ready equipment, and we come back and take everything down after. No stress on your end at all.

If budget is the main concern, the [lower-priced option] might be a great fit — it's $[price] and still gets the kids going wild. Want me to tell you more about it?
```

### "I need to think about it / talk to my partner"
```
Of course — take your time!

Just a heads up: [specific date] books up fast, especially in [month]. Once a date is gone, it's gone for the season.

If you decide you want to move forward, I can hold the date with just a deposit. No pressure at all — just want to make sure you don't miss out 🙏
```

### "Can you do better on the price?"
```
I hear you! Our pricing already includes full delivery, setup, and takedown — no hidden fees.

What I can do is throw in [one free lawn game / a small upgrade] if you book this week. I'd rather reward people who move quickly than discount everyone.

Does that work for you?
```

### "What if it rains?"
```
Great question! If there's heavy rain on your booking day, we work with you — we can reschedule to your next available date at no extra cost.

Light rain usually isn't an issue for covered setups, but we'd never want you running an event in unsafe conditions. Your satisfaction is the priority.
```

### "I haven't heard of you before"
```
Fair enough — we're a local Bridgewater business and we take a lot of pride in that.

Here's what a recent customer said: [paste real Google review]

We've served [X] families across the South Shore. Happy to give you the info of a past customer to call if that would help.
```

## Critical Rules
- **Always give a specific next step.** "Let me know if you're interested" is not a CTA. "Want me to send the booking form?" is.
- **Create urgency without lying.** Only say a date is filling up if it actually is.
- **Use their first name.** Every message.
- **Never let an inquiry sit more than 24 hours without a follow-up.**
- **Price objections = product mismatch.** Always offer a lower-priced alternative before giving up.
