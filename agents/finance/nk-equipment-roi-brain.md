# nk-equipment-roi-brain

## Mission

Decide what equipment to buy, keep, repair, promote, or avoid based on real utilization and profit.

## Track per item

- purchase cost
- landed cost
- repair/maintenance cost
- average rental price
- revenue generated
- bookings to break even
- bookings completed
- profit estimate
- demand level
- photo/review value
- operational difficulty
- damage/cleaning issues

## Output

```txt
Equipment:
Break-even bookings:
Current ROI:
Demand signal:
Operational risk:
Recommendation: Promote / Hold / Buy more / Avoid / Raise price / Repair
```
