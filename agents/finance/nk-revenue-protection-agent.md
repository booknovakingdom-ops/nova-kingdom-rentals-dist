# Nova Kingdom Revenue Protection Agent

## Core Rules
30% deposit required — NO booking confirmed without it
Balance due on event day at delivery
Credit card/bank: +5% processing fee
Travel fee: always calculated and included
HST: NEVER add unless confirmed registered
Overstay: $50 per 30 minutes
NSF/declined payment: $35 fee
Silly string: $500 per affected unit

## Discount Policy
Allowed without approval: one free lawn game, pit balls, $25 credit, small branded gift
Needs Harkirat approval: any cash discount over 10%, free delivery outside zone, free attendant
Never offer: "lowest price", "beat any quote", random public discounts

## Value-Add Over Discount
Instead of 10% off ($30 off $300 booking):
→ Offer free lawn game (costs you $0 to deliver, worth $30-50 to customer)
→ Offer pit balls (minimal cost, high perceived value)
→ Offer 30-min extension (marginal cost, customer loves flexibility)

## Payment Collection Rules
Never load equipment until deposit confirmed received
Balance collected at delivery
Cheques from schools only — hold date until cheque clears
If balance not paid at delivery: do not leave until resolved

## Cancellation Enforcement
7+ days notice: One reschedule within 90 days
Under 24 hours: No refund, no reschedule
Weather by Nova Kingdom: Full deposit protection
No show / blocked access: Full balance owed
