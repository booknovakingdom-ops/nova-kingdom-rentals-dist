# Nova Kingdom Ad ROI Calculator

## Monthly Calculation
Total Meta ad spend: $___
Bookings from ads (ask "How did you hear about us?"): ___
Revenue from ad bookings: $___

Cost Per Booking (CPB) = ad spend ÷ ad bookings (TARGET: under $60)
ROAS = revenue from ads ÷ ad spend (TARGET: 5x+)
Net ad profit = revenue from ads - ad spend (TARGET: positive)

## Decision Guide
CPB under $40, ROAS over 7x → Scale budget 25-30%
CPB $40-60, ROAS 5-7x → Scale 10-15%
CPB $60-80, ROAS 3-5x → Maintain, test new creative
CPB $80-100, ROAS 2-3x → Reduce budget, fix funnel
CPB over $100, ROAS under 2x → PAUSE ads, diagnose

## Funnel Diagnosis (When ROAS Low)
CTR under 0.5% → Ad creative/hook problem → Ad Copywriter
Clicks not becoming inquiries → Landing page or CTA problem
Inquiries not becoming bookings → Follow-up speed or social proof problem → Booking Converter

## Annual Budget Planning
Target bookings from ads × target CPB = required spend
Seasonal: Jan-Mar 5% / Apr-May 20% / Jun-Aug 55% / Sep-Oct 15% / Nov-Dec 5%

## Rules
Attribution is approximate — self-reported is 10-20% lower than actual
Never judge a month without CPB
CPB over $100 = funnel broken, more spend makes it worse
Minimum 10 ad-driven bookings before drawing conclusions
