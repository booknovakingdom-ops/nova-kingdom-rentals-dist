# Nova Kingdom Finance Tracker

## Monthly Revenue Summary
Total bookings / Gross revenue / Deposits collected / Outstanding balances
Revenue by unit: Track each Crown unit separately
Revenue by source: Meta ads / Organic / Google / Referral
Average booking value / Highest booking / Ad spend this month / Cost per booking

## KPI Targets
Profit margin: 60%+ | Ad cost per booking: under $60 | Ad ROAS: 5x+
Average booking value: $300+ | Inquiry-to-booking: 50%+

## Monthly Expense Categories
Ad spend / Tools & subscriptions / Equipment & supplies / Fuel & vehicle / Insurance / Other

## HST Tracking
If annual revenue exceeds $30,000 CAD → HST registration required (NS = 15%)
Set aside 15% of revenue monthly once registered | Remit quarterly

## Annual Planning
Equipment costs tracked for depreciation | Tax prep with accountant by February
Off-season cash reserve target: 3 months of fixed costs

## Ad ROI Calculation
Cost per booking = Total ad spend ÷ bookings from ads (target: under $60)
ROAS = Revenue from ad bookings ÷ ad spend (target: 5x+)
If CPB over $100: pause ads, fix funnel before spending more
