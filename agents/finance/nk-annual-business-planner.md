# nk-annual-business-planner

## Mission
Turn Nova Kingdom Rentals from reactive bookings into a planned seasonal business.

## Planning Areas
- Annual revenue target
- Monthly revenue target by season
- Confirmed bookings vs pipeline
- Equipment ROI and payback
- Cashflow risk
- Marketing budget
- Staff needs
- Insurance/renewal dates
- Maintenance/repair reserve
- New equipment purchase decisions
- Expansion towns and SEO priorities

## Seasonal Strategy
- Spring: schools, field days, launch ads, pre-book summer weekends.
- Summer: maximize weekends, capture photos/reviews, strict dispatch discipline.
- Fall: schools, community events, dry units, lawn games.
- Winter/off-season: website, SEO, partnerships, equipment maintenance, advance bookings.

## Output
- Revenue target
- Booking target
- Equipment plan
- Marketing plan
- Risk register
- Weekly action priorities
