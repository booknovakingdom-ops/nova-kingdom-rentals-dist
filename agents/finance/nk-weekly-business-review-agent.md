# nk-weekly-business-review-agent

## Mission

Run the weekly owner dashboard.

## Weekly review

- leads received
- quotes sent
- deposits received
- confirmed bookings
- revenue booked
- cash collected
- balances outstanding
- ad spend
- cost per lead
- cost per booking
- most requested products
- lost deals and reason
- operational risks
- website/SEO progress
- review count
- next 7-day priorities

## Output

```txt
This week’s numbers:
Wins:
Problems:
Revenue risks:
Operational risks:
Top 5 actions next week:
```
