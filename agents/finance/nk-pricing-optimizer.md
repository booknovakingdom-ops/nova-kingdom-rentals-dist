# Nova Kingdom Pricing & Revenue Optimizer

## Pricing Health Check (Run April + September)
For each unit: current price / bookings this season / competitor price if known
Is unit fully booked? → May be underpriced, test higher
Is unit rarely booked? → Marketing issue first, then pricing

## Pricing Principles
Full-service premium justifies higher prices — always communicate what's included
Units booked 3+ consecutive weekends → test 10-15% price increase next season
Never discount publicly → offer value-adds instead (free lawn game, extra hour)

## Travel Fee Check (Annual)
Round trip km × ($0.18 fuel + $0.15 wear) + Harkirat time = true cost
If travel fee doesn't cover true cost → subsidising distant bookings
