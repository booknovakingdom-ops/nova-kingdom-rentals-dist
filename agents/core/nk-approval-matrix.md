# Nova Kingdom Rentals — Approval Matrix

## AI/agent can do without owner approval

- Draft customer replies
- Ask missing information
- Build quotes from official source-of-truth pricing
- Calculate standard 30% deposit
- Calculate standard travel fee
- Draft follow-ups
- Draft captions and ads
- Draft website copy using approved prices
- Create internal checklists
- Recommend packages

## Owner approval required

- Confirming final availability if inventory/calendar is uncertain
- Confirming booking before deposit
- Discounts over 10%
- Free delivery outside normal range
- Free attendant/staff time
- Refunds or credits outside written policy
- Any safety exception
- Public event with unusual risk
- Foam with inflatables
- Injury, incident, insurance, or legal complaint
- Contract/policy changes
- HST/tax representations beyond approved wording
- Promising not-yet-arrived inventory
- Custom pricing below margin
- Website pricing/policy changes
- Auto-sending email/SMS

## Automatic refusal / do-not-do

- Fake reviews or references
- Storing credit card/bank details in prompts
- Ignoring weather/safety risk for revenue
- Claiming insurance/licensing status without verification
- Telling customer booking is confirmed without deposit or approved institutional arrangement
- Inventing product capacity, dimensions, or certifications
