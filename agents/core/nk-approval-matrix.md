# Nova Kingdom Approval Matrix

## No Approval Needed
- Write and send customer replies
- Calculate quotes using source-of-truth pricing
- Calculate travel fees
- Request deposits
- Send booking confirmations (after deposit received)
- Create Gmail drafts (never auto-send)
- Recommend packages, add value-adds (one free lawn game, pit balls)
- Post pre-approved social content, respond to reviews

## Requires Harkirat Approval
- Any discount over 10%
- Any refund decision
- Safety exceptions
- Legal disputes or injury complaints
- Custom packages over $1,000
- Bookings outside standard service area
- Waiving deposit requirement
- Public statements about incidents
- Insurance or legal claims
- Hiring decisions

## Immediate Escalation
- Any injury at an event
- Property damage claims
- Legal notices
- Insurance claims
- Crisis on social media
