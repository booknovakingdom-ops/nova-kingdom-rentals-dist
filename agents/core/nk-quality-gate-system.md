# Nova Kingdom Rentals — Quality Gate System

Quality gates prevent expensive mistakes. Use the relevant gate before sending or publishing.

## Quote quality gate

Before sending a quote, verify:

- Customer/event date known
- Address/town known or clearly requested
- Rental/event time known or clearly requested
- Correct product/package name
- Correct price from source of truth
- Travel calculated after first 15 km free
- Staff fee included if applicable
- Add-ons included if applicable
- 30% deposit calculated
- Balance calculated
- Payment method and 5% processing fee handled if relevant
- No HST mistake
- No booking confirmation before deposit
- Clear next step
- Tone matches channel

Fail if any price, deposit, availability, or delivery detail is guessed.

## Customer message quality gate

Before sending/drafting:

- Sounds human, not robotic
- Not too long for Messenger/Instagram
- Professional enough for school/organization
- Does not include owner’s personal name unless requested
- Does not mention internal notes
- Does not overpromise availability, licensing, insurance, or safety
- Includes address request when needed
- Includes next step

## Booking confirmation gate

Before saying confirmed:

- Deposit received OR approved institutional payment arrangement documented
- Date/time/address confirmed
- Item/package confirmed
- Total/deposit/balance confirmed
- Agreement/waiver sent or queued
- Calendar/CRM updated
- Dispatch task created

## Event safety gate

Before operating:

- Weather acceptable
- Wind acceptable
- No thunder/lightning risk at operating time
- Surface suitable
- Anchoring method confirmed: stakes outside, sandbags indoors/hard surfaces
- Power/water confirmed if needed
- Adult supervision confirmed or attendants booked
- Equipment clean/inspected
- Blower/cord/hose trip hazards controlled
- Emergency shutdown plan known

If safety fails, do not operate.

## Website quality gate

Before pushing website content:

- Prices correct
- Packages correct
- Product names correct
- Book Now/contact buttons work
- Phone/email links work
- Images load
- Mobile layout clean
- No unavailable inventory presented as guaranteed
- Delivery/deposit terms accurate
- SEO title/meta present
- No fake reviews or fake claims
- No spelling mistakes in visible headings

## Ad/campaign gate

Before launching ad:

- Clear offer
- Correct price/terms
- Correct CTA
- Target location makes sense
- Landing page/message flow ready
- Lead follow-up process ready
- Success metric is cost per qualified lead/deposit, not likes

## Finance/margin gate

Before approving discount/custom quote:

- Travel and labour included
- Fuel/time considered
- Wear-and-tear considered
- Payment fees considered
- Booking is worth the calendar slot
- Discount does not train customer to expect cheap pricing
- Value-add considered before discount

# Manager Review Gate

Before any customer-facing or live-business output, confirm:
- correct department manager reviewed employee work;
- source of truth was checked;
- cross-department review happened where needed;
- Quality Control Manager approved or listed corrections;
- owner approval is requested for high-risk decisions.

## Fail Conditions
Fail the output if:
- an employee agent bypassed manager review;
- quality control was skipped;
- a manager changed facts without source-of-truth support;
- there is unresolved conflict between Sales, Safety, Finance, Legal, or Operations;
- owner approval is required but not requested.



## Merge-Final Accuracy Gates

### Lawn Game Pricing Gate
- 5 lawn games standalone must be $150.
- Upgrade from included 5 games to all 12 must be +$100.
- Add all 12 when no games are included must be $250.
- Fail any quote using old +$150 upgrade or $300 all-12 pricing.

### Insurance / Trust Gate
- Insurance references must use $2M liability coverage, Broker Link 1408, and April 28 renewal/key date only when relevant.
- Do not claim an event-specific certificate exists unless verified.

### Launch Date Gate
- Reference request language must say Nova Kingdom Rentals launched in May 2026, not vague “this season” wording.

### Review Link Gate
- Review requests should include https://g.page/r/CZXOs7GUjxR5EBI/review unless intentionally asking for private NPS feedback first.

### Short-Event Pricing Gate
- 3-hour, 4-hour, 5-hour, and custom shortened pricing must be marked [TO BE CONFIRMED] unless an approved quote exists.
