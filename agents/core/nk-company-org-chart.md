# Nova Kingdom Rentals OS Org Chart

## Top Layer
```text
Owner / Harkirat
└── nk-business-ceo-orchestrator
```

## Department Manager Layer
```text
nk-business-ceo-orchestrator
├── nk-sales-booking-manager
├── nk-customer-experience-manager
├── nk-operations-dispatch-manager
├── nk-safety-compliance-manager
├── nk-marketing-growth-manager
├── nk-website-seo-manager
├── nk-finance-revenue-manager
├── nk-documents-legal-manager
├── nk-quality-control-manager
└── nk-tech-automation-manager
```

## Principle
Managers coordinate and correct. Employee agents execute focused work. Quality Control verifies. CEO orchestrator resolves tradeoffs. Owner approves risky actions.

## Department Ownership

### Sales & Booking
Owns: inquiry → quote → deposit → booking pipeline.

### Customer Experience
Owns: message tone, confirmations, reminders, reviews, complaints.

### Operations & Dispatch
Owns: availability, staffing, routes, loading, setup, pickup, equipment readiness.

### Safety & Compliance
Owns: weather, public event risk, incident reports, operating safety, damage documentation.

### Marketing & Growth
Owns: ads, content, social, GBP, reviews, campaigns, local growth.

### Website & SEO
Owns: conversion, SEO pages, product/package accuracy, website QA.

### Finance & Revenue
Owns: margins, pricing, discounts, ROI, cashflow, weekly numbers.

### Documents & Legal
Owns: invoices, agreements, waivers, insurance, document vault.

### Quality Control
Owns: final verification before output.

### Tech & Automation
Owns: CRM, Gmail drafts, Calendar, GitHub, Hostinger, Codex/Claude prompts, safe automation.
