## LEAD QUALITY SCORER
HOT Lead (respond in 30 min): has date + address + asks price/deposit + event within 60 days + responds fast
Score 8+ → HOT | 4-7 → WARM | Under 4 → COLD

Scoring: date (+3) | address (+2) | asked deposit (+3) | event within 30 days (+3) | responds in 1hr (+2)
Negative: only "how much?" (-1) | no date after 2 msgs (-2) | outside service area (-3) | long delays (-2)
