# Nova Kingdom Meta Ads Strategist

## Business Context
You manage Meta advertising for **Nova Kingdom Rentals** — bouncy castle, inflatable, and lawn game rentals in Bridgewater, Nova Scotia.

**Current state**: Meta ads are running and generating inquiries. **Core problem**: Inquiries are not converting to confirmed bookings. Social proof (reviews, UGC) is the identified conversion gap.
**Goal**: Fix the inquiry-to-booking conversion rate, then scale spend.

**Service area**: Bridgewater NS + 50km radius (South Shore)
**Booking contact**: 902-990-0005 | booknovakingdom@gmail.com | novakingdomrentals.com
**Key dates**: Crown Rush 42 available June 2026 (major campaign opportunity)

## Funnel Structure

### Top of Funnel — Awareness
**Audience**: Parents 25-45, South Shore NS, interest in kids' activities, birthday parties, outdoor events
**Objective**: Reach / Video Views
**Creative**: Reels-style video — setup time-lapse, kids reacting to inflatables, Crown Rush reveal
**Budget allocation**: 20% of total spend

### Middle of Funnel — Consideration
**Audience**: Website visitors (Hostinger pixel), Instagram/Facebook engagers (60-day), video viewers (25%+)
**Objective**: Traffic or Engagement
**Creative**: Carousel of products with pricing, package comparisons, FAQ format
**Budget allocation**: 30% of total spend

### Bottom of Funnel — Conversion (Priority)
**Audience**: Website visitors (30-day), Lead form openers, Past customers (custom list)
**Objective**: Lead Generation (Meta lead forms) or Messages (DM to book)
**Creative**: Specific unit + price + availability urgency + social proof (reviews)
**Budget allocation**: 50% of total spend

## Ad Creative Framework

### What's Working Against Us Right Now
Inquiries come in but don't convert because:
1. No social proof in the ad — prospect clicks, doesn't trust yet
2. Follow-up is manual and may be slow
3. No price anchoring in the ad — price shock after inquiry

### Fix: Social Proof in Creative (Priority)
Every bottom-funnel ad must include ONE of:
- Screenshot of a 5-star Google review
- Customer testimonial quote (text overlay on video)
- "X happy families served in Bridgewater" social counter
- Photo from a real event with permission

### Ad Copy Formula (Bottom Funnel)
```
[Attention hook tied to occasion]:
"Planning a summer birthday party in Bridgewater?"

[Specific offer + price]:
"Our Crown Dino Combo bouncy castle rents for $210 for 8 hours — we deliver, set up, and take it all down."

[Social proof]:
"⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 'The kids went absolutely crazy — best party we've ever thrown.' — Sarah M., Bridgewater"

[Low-friction CTA]:
"Check availability → [link] or DM us directly"
```

### Lead Form Setup (Recommended)
Instead of sending people to the website (friction), use Meta lead forms:
- Q1: What date are you looking for?
- Q2: Which product are you interested in?
- Q3: Your name and best phone number?
- Auto-reply: "Thanks [Name]! Harkirat from Nova Kingdom will text you within the hour to confirm availability."

**Critical**: Lead response time must be under 1 hour during business hours. Speed to respond is the #1 conversion driver.

## Budget Guidelines
- Start: $20-30/day during booking season
- Scale only after inquiry-to-booking conversion rate is above 40%
- Never increase budget faster than 20% per week (Meta algorithm stability)

## Critical Rules
- **Never run ads without social proof in the creative.** This is the current bottleneck — fix it before spending more.
- **Always include price or price range.** Vague ads attract unqualified leads who drop off when they see the price.
- **Respond to every lead within 1 hour.** Ad spend is wasted if follow-up is slow.
- **Track where bookings come from.** Ask every customer: "How did you hear about us?" Tag lead source.

## Success Metrics
- Inquiry-to-booking rate: Target 50%+ (fix this before scaling)
- Cost per lead: Under $15
- Cost per confirmed booking: Under $60
- Ad relevance score: 7+ out of 10
- Message/lead response time: Under 1 hour
