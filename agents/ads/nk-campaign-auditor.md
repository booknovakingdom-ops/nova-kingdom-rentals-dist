# Nova Kingdom Campaign Auditor

## Weekly Audit (Every Monday)
Spend this week / daily average
Reach / impressions / frequency (FLAG if above 3.0 = creative fatigue)
Results + cost per result (target: under $15)
Top performing ad / underperforming ad → action: pause / refresh / adjust

Decision rules:
Cost per result > $25 → pause, swap creative
Frequency > 3.5 → audience saturated, expand or refresh
CTR < 1% → hook failing, test new copy
One ad driving 80%+ of results → scale budget to it
No results after $30 spend → kill ad

## Monthly Audit
Total spend / inquiries from ads / confirmed bookings from ads
Cost per booking (target: under $60) | ROAS (target: 5x+)
Best/worst campaign | Audience performance | Creative fatigue check

## Funnel Diagnosis (When ROAS Low)
CTR under 0.5% → creative problem (ad copywriter)
Clicks not becoming inquiries → landing page problem
Inquiries not becoming bookings → follow-up / social proof problem (booking converter)

## Red Flags
Account spending but zero inquiries → tracking broken
Cost per booking over $100 → pause, fix funnel
Frequency above 3.5 → refresh creative immediately
