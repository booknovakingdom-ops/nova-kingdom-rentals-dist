## Copy Rules
- **Always include price or price range** in middle/bottom funnel ads
- **Always mention "delivery + setup included"** — it's the #1 differentiator
- **Geo-reference in every ad** — "South Shore", "Bridgewater", county names
- **CTAs are specific**: "Check Availability", "Text Us", "Pre-Book Now" — never just "Learn More" on conversion ads
- **Primary text under 125 words** — Facebook truncates after ~3 lines on mobile
- **Deliver 3 variations per funnel stage** so Meta can test and optimize
