## Birthday & Anniversary Reminders

The single highest-converting CRM action: reaching out before a customer's annual event.

**Setup**: After every booking, log the event type and approximate date.

**Pre-event outreach (6 weeks before estimated event date):**
```
Hey [Name]! Harkirat from Nova Kingdom here 👋

Hard to believe it's almost been a year since [kid's name]'s [birthday/event]! 

Are you planning anything this [month/summer]? I wanted to reach out early since [June/July] weekends fill up fast.

Would love to have you back — I can hold your date with just a deposit 😊

— Harkirat | 902-990-0005
```

## Customer Tiers

### Standard (1 booking)
- Normal follow-up sequence
- Review request after booking

### Loyal (2+ bookings OR $500+ spend)
- Priority date holds (first pick before public)
- Mention loyalty: "As someone who's booked with us before, I want to give you first access"
- Birthday/event reminder outreach

### VIP (4+ bookings OR $1,500+ lifetime spend)
- Direct Harkirat contact (personal cell)
- Small appreciation gesture when possible (thank-you card, small freebie)
- First access to new units (Crown Rush 42 pre-launch)
- "You've been one of our best customers — I want to make sure you're always taken care of"

## CRM Tools (Recommended)

**Starting out (free/cheap):**
- Google Sheets with the template above — manually maintained
- Phone contacts with detailed notes per customer

**When scaling (50+ customers):**
- **HoneyBook** — built for service businesses, handles booking + CRM
- **Jobber** — popular for field service/rental businesses
- **Airtable** — flexible database with automation

---

## Critical Rules
- **Log every new customer within 24 hours of their first booking.**
- **Never rely on memory for repeat customer outreach.** Set calendar reminders for every annual event.
- **Referrals always get acknowledged.** Every time. Even a text.
- **Lifetime value is the metric that matters.** A customer who books 3 times is worth more than 3 different one-time customers.
- **Privacy**: Customer data stays internal. Never share customer contact info with third parties.
