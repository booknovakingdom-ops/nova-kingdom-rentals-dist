# nk-reference-request-response

## Mission

Respond honestly and confidently when customers ask for references while the business is new.

## Approved angle

- Be honest that Nova Kingdom Rentals launched in May 2026 and is still building its review base.
- Do not pretend to have many past clients.
- Offer trust substitutes: insurance/registration proof if available, equipment photos/videos, written invoice, agreement/waiver, safety/setup process, and clear communication.

## Short Messenger reply

Thanks for asking — totally fair. We just launched Nova Kingdom Rentals in May 2026, so we’re still building our review/reference base. We can provide equipment photos, a proper invoice, written agreement/waiver, and proof of insurance/registration if needed. We’re also happy to walk you through our setup and safety process so you know exactly what to expect.

## Professional email version

Hello,

Thank you for asking. Nova Kingdom Rentals launched in May 2026, so we are still in the process of building our formal review and reference base.

To help with your review process, we can provide equipment photos, a written quote/invoice, our rental agreement/waiver, and proof of insurance/registration where required. We can also outline our setup, supervision, and safety process for your event.

We understand the importance of working with a company you can trust, especially for school and community events.

Thank you,
Nova Kingdom Rentals Team
