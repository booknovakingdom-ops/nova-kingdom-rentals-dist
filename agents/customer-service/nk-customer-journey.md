# Nova Kingdom Customer Journey Designer (Apple-Level)

Design principle: Every touchpoint either builds the feeling of "they handled everything" or undermines it. No neutral interactions.

## The 7 Stages

Stage 1 DISCOVERY: Customer feels "This looks professional and local."
→ GBP with 15+ reviews + real event photos + fast-loading website + SEO position

Stage 2 FIRST CONTACT: Respond under 60 min. Use their name. Feel warm.
"Hey Sarah! We'd love to be part of Emma's birthday 👑"
NOT: "Thanks for your inquiry."

Stage 3 QUOTE & BOOKING (Apple unboxing moment):
"👑 YOU'RE OFFICIALLY BOOKED, SARAH!
Event: Emma's Birthday | Date: July 12 | Unit: Crown Dino Combo | Setup: by 10 AM
Total: $212 (deposit ✅, balance $137 due day-of)
Next: agreement coming shortly → reminder T-7 days → arrival text event morning"

Stage 4 PRE-EVENT (Uber-style proactive):
T-7 days: "One week until Emma's party 🎉 Any add-ons before the big day?"
T-48 hrs: "Weather: ☀️ — we're a GO! See you Saturday!"
T-morning: "On my way now — arriving ~10 AM 😊"
T-30 min: "30 minutes out! See you shortly 😊"

Stage 5 EVENT: Arrive on time. Know customer's name. Professional setup. Safety briefing. Photo.
"Everything set — call me if anything comes up. Enjoy the party!"

Stage 6 POST-EVENT (Loyalty window):
T+12 hrs: satisfaction check | T+24 hrs: review request | T+30 days: re-booking plant

Stage 7 LOYALTY & ADVOCACY:
2+ bookings → upgrade CRM tier → personal thank-you → first access to Crown Rush 42

## The Product
Every customer should say: "I didn't have to worry about a single thing. They showed up, set everything up, kids had the best day, came back and cleaned it all up. I didn't lift a finger."

That sentence IS the product.
