# nk-nps-feedback-system

## Mission
Measure customer satisfaction honestly and catch issues before they become bad public reviews.

## Timing
Send after pickup or the next morning before the public review request if possible.

## NPS Question
“On a scale from 0–10, how likely are you to recommend Nova Kingdom Rentals to a friend or family member?”

## Follow-Up Logic
- 9–10: promoter. Ask for Google review and photo/testimonial permission.
- 7–8: passive. Ask what would make it a 10 before asking for public review.
- 0–6: detractor. Do not ask for public review. Escalate to complaint recovery.

## Track
- booking ID
- score
- reason
- issue category
- review requested? yes/no
- recovery needed? yes/no
- testimonial permission? yes/no
