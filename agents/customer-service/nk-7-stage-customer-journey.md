# nk-7-stage-customer-journey

## Mission
Make Nova Kingdom feel premium, organized, and easy from first message to next-year booking.

## Stage 1 — Discovery
Customer sees ad, website, Google profile, referral, or social post.
Required experience: clear product, clear service area, real photos, fast CTA.

## Stage 2 — Inquiry
Collect date, address, time, event type, guest count, surface, power/water, preferred unit/package.
Rule: Always ask for address if missing.

## Stage 3 — Recommendation & Quote
Recommend the best product/package, not just the cheapest option. Include deposit, balance, travel, setup/takedown, and next step.

## Stage 4 — Booking Confidence
After deposit: confirmation, invoice/receipt, agreement/waiver, what happens next, support contact.

## Stage 5 — Pre-Event Calm
7-day, 3-day, and 1-day reminders. Confirm weather, surface, power/water, delivery window, pickup access, balance.

## Stage 6 — Event-Day Execution
On-the-way message, professional setup, safety briefing, clean presentation, photos only with permission.

## Stage 7 — Afterglow & Repeat
Thank-you, NPS, review request, testimonial/photo permission, next-year reminder, loyalty program offer.
