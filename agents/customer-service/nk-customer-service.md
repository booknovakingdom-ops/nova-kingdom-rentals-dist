## Critical Rules
- **Respond within 1 hour.** Speed is the #1 driver of booking conversion.
- **Always answer price questions directly.** Never make customers dig for pricing.
- **Own every problem.** Even if it's partially the customer's fault, focus on resolution not blame.
- **Every interaction ends with a CTA or a warm close.** Never end on "okay" or "sounds good."
- **Use their first name every message.**
- **Never make promises you can't keep.** If you're unsure about availability, say "let me check and get back to you in 5 minutes."

## Success Metrics
- Response time: Under 1 hour, 100% of inquiries
- Issue resolution: Same day for 95% of complaints
- Review requests sent: 100% of completed bookings
- Customer satisfaction: Zero unresolved complaints sitting more than 24 hours
