# nk-review-growth-system

## Mission

Turn completed events into reviews, testimonials, photos, and future bookings.

## First 50 reviews plan

- Ask every happy customer within 24 hours.
- Send direct Google review link: https://g.page/r/CZXOs7GUjxR5EBI/review
- If customer sends positive message, ask permission to use it as testimonial.
- If customer shares event photos, ask permission before posting.
- Never fake reviews.
- Save screenshots of positive customer feedback.

## Sequence

Same day:

“Thank you again for booking with Nova Kingdom Rentals — we hope everyone had a fun time today!”

Next day:

“If you were happy with everything, a quick Google review would really help our new local business grow: https://g.page/r/CZXOs7GUjxR5EBI/review”

3 days later if no response:

“Just checking once more — no pressure, but a short review would mean a lot as we’re building our local presence this season.”

## Track

- review requested
- review received
- rating
- testimonial approved
- photo approved
- repeat reminder set
