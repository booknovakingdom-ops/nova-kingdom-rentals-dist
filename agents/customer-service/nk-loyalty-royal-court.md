# nk-loyalty-royal-court

## Mission
Increase repeat bookings without training customers to expect random discounts.

## Core Principle
Prefer loyalty perks and value-adds over cutting price.

## Suggested Tiers

### Knight
For first-time completed customers.
Perks: early access to new units, review/photo thank-you perk when approved.

### Baron
For 2 completed bookings or referred customer.
Perks: priority booking window, small add-on perk when available.

### Earl
For 3+ bookings or school/community repeat partner.
Perks: priority dates, package upgrade consideration, annual event reminder.

### Duke
For major repeat partners, schools, municipalities, large annual events.
Perks: priority planning call, custom quote support, early seasonal booking access.

## Rules
- Do not promise automatic cash discounts.
- Owner approval required for discounts over 10% or free delivery outside rules.
- Track referral source and repeat customer status in CRM.
