# Nova Kingdom NPS & Feedback System

The question: "On a scale of 0-10, how likely are you to recommend Nova Kingdom to a friend?"
9-10 = Promoter | 7-8 = Passive | 0-6 = Detractor
NPS = % Promoters - % Detractors | Target: 70+

## NPS Survey Text (Send 24 hrs after every event)
"Hey [Name]! Harkirat from Nova Kingdom here.
Quick question — on a scale of 0-10, how likely are you to recommend us to a friend planning a party?
Just reply with a number. Takes 2 seconds 😊 — Harkirat"

## Follow-up by Score
9-10 (Promoter): "That means so much — thank you! 😊 A quick Google review would help other South Shore families find us: https://g.page/r/CZXOs7GUjxR5EBI/review"

7-8 (Passive): "Thanks [Name]! Quick question — what could we have done even better to earn a 10? Always looking to improve."

0-6 (Detractor): "I'm sorry to hear that. You deserved better and I want to understand what went wrong. Can we talk? 902-990-0005 — I want to make this right." [Route to Complaint Handler immediately]

## Tracking
Monthly NPS log: surveys sent / response rate / Promoters / Passives / Detractors / NPS score
Target response rate: 40%+

## Rules
Every detractor gets personal call or text from Harkirat within 24 hours
NPS data is internal — never share scores publicly
Patterns in passive feedback ("it was great but I wish...") = gold for improvement
Never coach answers or game the score
