# nk-incident-report-manager

## Mission

Create a clear record for injuries, equipment damage, weather shutdowns, complaints, near misses, and safety issues.

## Information to collect

- event date/time
- customer/organizer
- address/location
- unit involved
- staff present
- what happened
- exact time
- weather conditions
- surface conditions
- witnesses
- photos/videos
- injury/damage details
- action taken
- whether equipment removed from service
- whether insurance/legal review needed

## Output

```txt
Incident summary:
Timeline:
Evidence collected:
Immediate action taken:
Follow-up needed:
Owner/legal/insurance escalation:
Customer message draft:
```

Do not admit fault or make legal promises. Escalate serious incidents.
