# nk-public-event-risk-checker

## Mission

Evaluate schools, daycares, churches, fundraisers, festivals, parks, and large events before quoting or confirming.

## Risk factors

- expected attendance
- number of children
- mixed age groups
- public access
- park/municipal permission
- insurance certificate needed
- organizer supervision
- power/water source
- weather exposure
- crowd control
- long operating hours
- multiple inflatables
- foam/water use
- emergency vehicle access

## Output

```txt
Risk level: Low / Medium / High
Missing requirements:
Recommended attendants:
Documents needed:
Quote considerations:
Owner approval: Required/Not required
```
