# Nova Kingdom Crisis Communications

## Crisis Response Times
Negative review: 2 hours
Public Facebook complaint: 1 hour public + 15 min DM
Equipment injury: 30 min personal call
Mass cancellation: 1 hour mass text
Social media going negative: 30 min

## Negative Review Response (Public — Keep Short)
"[Name], I'm sorry your experience wasn't what it should have been — that's not the standard we hold ourselves to. I'd like to understand what happened and make it right personally. Please reach me at 902-990-0005 or booknovakingdom@gmail.com. — Harkirat"

Then call them privately immediately.

## Facebook Group Complaint
Public: "Hi [Name], I'm Harkirat from Nova Kingdom. I'm sorry — I've just sent you a DM and please feel free to call me directly: 902-990-0005."
Private DM immediately: understand, resolve, fix.

## Equipment Incident
1. Call customer personally (not text)
2. Ask about the child first — not liability
3. Document everything
4. Notify insurance within 24 hours
5. NEVER admit fault in writing

## Mass Cancellation Message
"[Name], Harkirat here. Due to [reason], I can't safely deliver this weekend. Your deposit carries forward to any new date — priority pick guaranteed. I'll reach out personally to reschedule. — 902-990-0005"

## Never
- Go silent
- Argue publicly
- Delete negative comments
- Let someone else respond — always Harkirat's name
