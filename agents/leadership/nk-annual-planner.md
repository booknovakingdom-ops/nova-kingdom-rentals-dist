# Nova Kingdom Annual Business Planner

Run in September/October after peak season.

## Year in Review Template
Total bookings / revenue / best month / worst month
Average booking value / most booked unit
Bookings by channel (ads/organic/Google/referral)
Ad spend / cost per booking / ROI
Review count start vs end of year
What worked / what didn't / what was left on table

## Revenue Target Formula
Last year revenue × growth target % = target
Target ÷ average booking value = bookings needed
Bookings needed ÷ peak months = bookings per month

## Quarterly Milestones
Q1 (Jan-Mar): Build pipeline, school outreach, Crown Rush prep
Q2 (Apr-Jun): Scale ads, Crown Rush launch, review push
Q3 (Jul-Sep): Execute perfectly, capture UGC, maximize upsells
Q4 (Oct-Dec): Debrief, plan next year, early bookings

## Equipment Acquisition Decision
Purchase cost ÷ average revenue per booking = break-even bookings
Only buy when current units are 80%+ utilized in peak season

## Expansion Triggers
Turn down 2+ bookings/month → hire assistant
20%+ inquiries from outside area → expand service area
80%+ utilization → acquire new unit
