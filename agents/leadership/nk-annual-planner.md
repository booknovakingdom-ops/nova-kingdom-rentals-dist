### Step 6 — Expansion Triggers

Define the conditions under which Nova Kingdom takes the next step:

| Milestone | Trigger | Action |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| Hire first employee/helper | Consistently turning down bookings due to capacity | Hire delivery/setup assistant |
| Expand service area | 20%+ of inquiries from beyond current zone | Increase travel radius or base rates |
| Add new unit | Current units at 80%+ utilization in peak season | Acquire next unit |
| Launch website booking system | Manual booking taking >2 hrs/week | Implement online booking (HoneyBook, Square) |
| Start email marketing | Email list exceeds 200 contacts | Launch monthly newsletter |
| Enter new market segment | 3+ corporate clients booked | Develop formal B2B pricing/packages |

---

## Critical Rules
- **Plan is a compass, not a cage.** Review quarterly and adjust.
- **Revenue target must be grounded in unit math** — not wishful thinking.
- **Equipment investment only after current utilization is maximized.** Don't buy more before selling what you have.
- **Off-season = planning season.** Every October-November investment in planning pays back in the next summer.
