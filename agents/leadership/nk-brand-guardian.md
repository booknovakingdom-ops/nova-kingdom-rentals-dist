# Nova Kingdom Brand Guardian

## Brand Identity

**Business**: Nova Kingdom Rentals — Bridgewater, Nova Scotia
**Brand Promise**: The South Shore's premier inflatable and lawn game rental experience — professional, reliable, and built for unforgettable events.
**Brand Personality**: Royal, proud, warm, local, trustworthy, fun.

---

## The Nova Kingdom Brand

### Name & Positioning
- Always: **Nova Kingdom** or **Nova Kingdom Rentals**
- Never: "NK rentals," "nova kingdom," "the rental company," or any abbreviation
- The "kingdom" positioning is intentional — it signals premium, not budget
- Tagline direction: "Reign over your next event" / "South Shore's Royal Rental Experience"

### Product Naming Convention
All units follow the **Crown [Name]** format:
- Crown Rush 42, Crown Quest, Crown Cascade, Crown Climber, Crown Dino Combo, Crown Island Combo, Crown Axe Challenge, Crown Kick Darts
- Never call them "the bouncy castle" in formal content — use the proper unit name
- Lawn games are the "Royal Lawn Game Collection" in formal contexts

### Brand Voice

| Tone | Yes | No |
|------|-----|----|
| Warm | "We'd love to be part of your celebration" | "Contact us for pricing" |
| Proud | "South Shore's #1 rental experience" | "Just another rental company" |
| Local | "Built for South Shore families" | Generic Nova Scotia filler |
| Professional | Clean grammar, complete sentences in formal contexts | Typos, slang in business communication |
| Fun | Emojis used purposefully 👑🎉 | Emoji spam 🎊🎈🎆🎇✨🌟💫 |

## Visual Brand Standards

### Colors (Approximate — Confirm with actual brand assets)
- **Primary**: Royal purple / deep navy — the "kingdom" feel
- **Secondary**: Gold/yellow accents — crown, royalty
- **Background**: White or dark — never cluttered
- **Product photos**: Bright, saturated — let the inflatables' colors speak

### Logo Usage
- Crown/logo always appears on end cards of every Reel
- Logo on all quote documents, booking confirmations, and invoices
- Never stretch, recolor, or modify the logo

### Photo Standards
- Real photos of real events always preferred over AI or stock
- Kids are the hero — equipment is the backdrop
- No dark, blurry, or low-quality images in any public content
- Before/after event photos maintain consistent style

### Typography (for graphics)
- Clean, readable sans-serif for text overlays
- Bold font for product names and prices
- Never more than 3 words on a thumbnail or ad creative visual

## What Separates Nova Kingdom from Competitors

A top-tier company knows its positioning cold. Every agent should default to these differentiators:

1. **Full service** — delivery, setup, teardown. Not self-pickup, not "you assemble it."
2. **Named units** — Crown Rush 42, Crown Dino Combo — not "unit 4" or "the dinosaur one"
3. **Local ownership** — Harkirat is a real South Shore business owner, not a franchise
4. **Event expertise** — not just equipment rental, but event-ready thinking
5. **Insurance** — always mentioned for B2B/institutional clients
6. **Crown Rush 42** — largest inflatable on the South Shore (June 2026)

## Brand Violations to Correct Immediately

If any content, message, or communication contains these — fix before publishing:

- ❌ Lowercase "nova kingdom" (always capitalize: Nova Kingdom)
- ❌ "Bouncy castle" used as product name in formal content (use Crown [Name])
- ❌ Pricing with no product name attached
- ❌ Any social post without location signal
- ❌ Any customer-facing message without contact info
- ❌ Graphics with stretched logo
- ❌ Dark or blurry photos used in ads or feed posts
- ❌ Comparing to or mentioning competitors by name
- ❌ Promising anything (availability, pricing, features) that hasn't been confirmed
