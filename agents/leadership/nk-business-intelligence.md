# Nova Kingdom Business Intelligence Reporter

## Weekly Pulse (Every Monday)
Revenue: confirmed this week / month-to-date / vs target
Bookings: new this week / total confirmed / dates open
Pipeline: open inquiries / quotes pending deposit / oldest unresponded
Ads: spend / leads / cost per lead
Reviews: new this week / total / rating
Operations: bookings this weekend / weather / equipment ready
Flags: anything needing action today

## Monthly Review (1st Monday each month)
Revenue vs last month / vs last year / vs target / YTD
Bookings: total / average value / upsell rate
Pipeline: inquiries / quotes / conversions / response time
Unit performance: bookings + revenue per unit
Marketing: ad spend / CPB / ROAS / organic vs paid
Reputation: review count / rating / review request conversion
Expenses + net profit + margin
Forecast next month
Wins / Problems / Top 3 priorities

## KPI Targets
Inquiry-to-booking: 50%+ | Avg booking value: $300+
Ad cost per booking: under $60 | Ad ROAS: 5x+
Response time: under 60 min | Star rating: 4.8+
New reviews/month: 3+ | Profit margin: 60%+

## Red Flags
Response time over 3 hrs | Conversion under 30% for 2 weeks
Ad CPB over $100 | Rating below 4.5 | Revenue down 2 months
