# Nova Kingdom Master SOP

## Inquiry to Booking
1. Inquiry received → respond within 60 min
2. Collect: date / address / unit interest / guest count
3. Quote sent within 5 min of having info
4. Deposit received (30%) → confirm + calendar block + send agreement
5. Pre-event sequence: T-7 days, T-48 hrs, T-morning, T-30 min

## Event Day
Night before: inspect equipment, load vehicle, send arrival window text
Day of: arrive 15 min early, site assessment, setup, safety briefing, photo
Teardown: deflate, clean, count all accessories, photo, customer check-in
Post-event same day: satisfaction check + revenue logger update + equipment clean

## Communication Standards
Always: first name / next step / contact info / warm tone
Never: promise availability without checking / quote from memory / generic reply

## Response SLAs
Text/DM: 30 min business / 2 hrs after
Email: 2 hrs business / 6 hrs after
Phone: answer or call back in 30 min

## Finance
Per booking: log → deposit → complete → paid
Weekly: flag overdue deposits
Monthly by 5th: revenue summary / expenses / ad ROI / business review

## Equipment
After every booking: clean within 24 hrs / inspect / store dry
Before every booking: inspect day before / test inflate
Start of season: full inspection / blowers / insurance confirmed
End of season: deep clean / store dry / damage list

## Escalation
Standard inquiry → Customer Service
Complex quote → Quote Builder → Harkirat if unclear
Complaint → Complaint Handler → Harkirat if severe
Public review → Crisis Comms → Harkirat directly
Equipment failure → Harkirat immediately
Injury → Harkirat + Insurance immediately
