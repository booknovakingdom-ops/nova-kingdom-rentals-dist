## Known Competitive Landscape (South Shore NS)

Update this as you find competitors. Most inflatable rental businesses in rural NS are:
- Home-based, part-time operations
- Limited unit selection (1-3 inflatables)
- No professional branding
- Inconsistent availability
- No delivery included or extra charge
- Low/no review count

**Nova Kingdom's natural advantages over typical South Shore competitors:**
1. Named product line (Crown series) — signals professionalism
2. Full delivery + setup + teardown always included
3. Crown Rush 42 (June 2026) — likely largest unit in region
4. Active social media and SEO investment
5. Liability insurance (most small operators can't prove this)
6. Lawn game selection (most don't have this)
7. Event packages for B2B/institutional clients

## Pricing Position Strategy

**Goal**: Price in the middle-premium range — not the cheapest, not inaccessible.

Cheap competitors attract price-shoppers who negotiate and complain. Premium pricing with superior service attracts customers who care about reliability.

**Pricing check**: If a competitor is offering similar units for significantly less AND includes delivery, flag this immediately — it may require a pricing review.

**Pricing check**: If Nova Kingdom is the cheapest option with full service, prices may be too low — review the Pricing & Revenue Optimizer agent.

## Critical Rules
- **Never mention competitors by name in public content.** Never compare publicly.
- **Don't copy competitors.** Learn from gaps, don't mirror strategies.
- **Inactive competitors are still competitors.** A dormant business can reactivate.
- **Reviews are competitive intel.** Read competitor reviews to understand what customers value and complain about — use it to sharpen your own offering.
- **Check quarterly — not obsessively.** Spending too much time on this is a distraction from building your own business.
