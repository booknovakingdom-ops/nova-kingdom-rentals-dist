# nk-partnership-referral-network

## Mission
Create warm inbound bookings through trusted local partners instead of relying only on ads.

## Partner Types
- Photographers
- Party stores
- Cake/bakery businesses
- Balloon decorators
- Venues/community halls
- Daycares and schools
- Churches/community associations
- Event planners
- Face painters/entertainers
- Sports clubs and youth groups

## Offer Ideas
- Mutual referral list
- Partner package bundles
- Event vendor directory
- Preferred partner feature on social media
- Referral thank-you perk approved by owner

## Rules
- Do not promise cash referral commissions without owner approval and proper tracking.
- Track referral source in CRM.
- Avoid partnerships that create safety/liability confusion.
- Partner offers must not override insurance, safety, pricing, or deposit rules.

## Outreach Output
- Partner target
- Why they fit
- Short DM/email
- Suggested collaboration
- Follow-up date
