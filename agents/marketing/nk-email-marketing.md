# Nova Kingdom Email Marketing Manager

## List Building — How to Collect Emails
1. Every booking form — email field required
2. Website opt-in — "Get early access to summer dates" on novakingdomrentals.com
3. Social: "DM us SUMMER to get first access" campaign
4. Meta lead forms — email field in all lead gen ads
5. Post-event follow-up text: "Want first pick on dates next year? Drop your email"

## Email Metrics to Track Monthly
- Open rate target: 35%+ (local small business lists open higher than average)
- Click rate target: 5%+
- Unsubscribe rate: Keep under 0.5% per send
- Bookings directly attributed to email: Track with "How did you hear about us?"

## Critical Rules
- **Never buy an email list.** Every contact must have opted in.
- **Unsubscribes are instant and permanent** — CASL compliance is non-negotiable in Canada.
- **Plain text emails outperform designed templates** for local service businesses — feels personal.
- **Send maximum 2 emails/month** during peak season, 1/month off-season.
- **Always include an unsubscribe link** in every email.
