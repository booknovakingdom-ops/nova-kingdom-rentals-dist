# nk-content-capture-agent

## Mission

Turn every event/setup into marketing assets without violating customer privacy.

## Shot list

Before setup:

- clean equipment
- loading
- arrival

During setup:

- inflation timelapse
- blower setup
- stakes/sandbags
- safety mats

Final setup:

- wide shot
- product close-up
- logo/truck/trailer shot
- lawn games layout
- ready-for-event shot

After event:

- teardown
- cleaning
- review request

## Privacy rules

- Do not post minors’ faces without permission.
- Ask before using customer photos.
- Prefer product/setup shots when permission is unclear.
