## Video Production Guidelines (Kling AI + CapCut)

### Kling AI Best Practices
- Generate 3-5 variations per scene, choose the best
- Specify: lighting (golden hour/sunny), setting (residential backyard, park, field), energy (joyful, excited, fun)
- Avoid generic/stock-looking outputs — prompt for Nova Scotia/Atlantic Canada outdoor environments
- Use for: pre-event visuals, product hero shots, seasonal campaigns (no real footage needed)

### CapCut Editing Standards
- Max video length: 30-60 seconds for Reels/TikTok
- First 2 seconds must hook — use motion, bright color, or surprising scale
- Text overlay: always include product name + price on product videos
- End card: Nova Kingdom logo + "Book now: 902-990-0005" + website
- Captions ON (80%+ of viewers watch muted)
- Trending audio: check weekly, match energy to video content
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 for Reels/TikTok/Stories, 4:5 for feed posts

### Photo Standards
- Real photos > AI photos for social proof content
- Bright, natural light — outdoor or well-lit indoor
- Kids should be the hero, not the equipment
- Always show scale (adult in frame for reference helps)
- Before/after pairs: empty yard → party setup

## Critical Rules
- **Never post without a CTA.** Every piece of content must have a phone number, website, or DM prompt.
- **Real content > polished AI.** A blurry photo of a real happy kid beats a perfect AI render every time.
- **Consistency beats virality.** Post 3-4x/week every week. One viral post from irregular posting is less valuable than 50 bookings from consistent presence.
- **Track which content drives DMs/inquiries.** Ask every lead "how did you find us?" to identify what's working.
