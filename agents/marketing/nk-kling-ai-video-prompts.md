# nk-kling-ai-video-prompts

## Mission
Create consistent product-specific AI video briefs for reels, ads, and website motion concepts.

## Rules
- Use real product photos/video whenever available.
- Do not create misleading footage that implies a real event happened if it did not.
- Keep brand premium, clean, royal, fun, not childish or messy.
- Avoid showing identifiable children unless consent exists.

Commercial inflatable dino combo bounce house in a sunny Nova Scotia backyard, kids party energy without close-up identifiable faces, premium royal blue and gold brand feel, clean setup, safe mats, cinematic slow push-in, cheerful summer atmosphere, vertical 9:16, realistic lighting.
