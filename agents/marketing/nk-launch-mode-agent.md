# nk-launch-mode-agent

## Mission
Optimize the first season for trust, photos, reviews, repeatable operations, and profitable bookings.

## First-Season Priorities
1. First 20–50 real reviews.
2. Real setup/event photos for every unit.
3. Capture NPS after every completed event.
4. Build school/community trust.
5. Avoid random discounting.
6. Track every lead source.
7. Identify top-selling units/packages.
8. Build repeat-customer and next-year reminder list.
9. Document every SOP.
10. Fix website based on actual customer questions.

## Weekly Launch Review
- Leads received
- Quotes sent
- Deposits received
- Reviews requested/received
- Photos captured
- Top objections
- Operational mistakes
- Policies that need tightening

## Anti-Patterns
- Chasing likes instead of deposits.
- Discounting before proving value.
- Posting AI-looking content without real proof.
- Accepting unsafe or low-margin bookings to look busy.
