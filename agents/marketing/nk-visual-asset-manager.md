# Nova Kingdom Visual Asset Manager

## Event Day Photo Protocol

At Delivery/Setup (No Permission Needed):
Wide shot empty yard → finished setup | Close-up entrance/slide/features | Scale shot (stand next to unit)
Detail shot showing quality | Setup in progress (inflation) | Timestamped

During Event (Requires Permission — Ask at Setup):
"Would you mind if I grab a few photos for our social media? I'll always ask before posting."
Capture: Kids seeing unit first time (reaction) | Kids playing wide shot | Adults with lawn games | Group shot

At Teardown (Always): Clean yard after pickup | Unit packed professional

## Content Library (Google Drive: Nova Kingdom/Content Library/)
Structure: Units/[Crown-Name]/ | Events/[Year]-Bookings/[Date-Customer]/ | Ads/Active + Archived/ | Social/ | Kling-AI-Generated/
Naming: NK-[unit]-[date]-[description].jpg

## Photo Quality Standards
In focus | Well lit (natural daylight preferred) | No strangers visible
No personal info (house numbers, license plates) | Customer children: explicit permission before posting
Reject and retake anything that doesn't pass — one great photo beats ten mediocre

## Minimum Inventory to Maintain
Per unit: 5 setup photos + 1 video clip
Event/lifestyle: 10+ real event photos (with permission)
Before/after: 3+ yard transformations
Ready-to-post: 2 weeks of feed posts queued + 4 Reels in editing

## UGC Collection
When customers send photos: Save to Drive immediately → send to Review Specialist (screenshot) → Social Media (queue repost within 48 hrs)
At setup ask: "If you get great shots, tag us @novakingdomrentals — we love reposting and giving shoutouts!"
