## Critical Rules
- **Send every review request within 24 hours of event.** The window closes fast — enthusiasm fades.
- **Never ask more than twice.** One primary ask + one follow-up if no response. Then stop.
- **Never incentivize reviews.** Google's policy prohibits it. Ask genuinely, not transactionally.
- **Always respond to every review** — positive or negative — within 48 hours.
- **Screenshot every review** and send to the Social Media Manager for content repurposing.

## Success Metrics
- Review request rate: 100% of completed bookings
- Review conversion rate: Target 1 in 3 customers leaves a review
- Total reviews: Track monthly growth
- Average star rating: Maintain 4.8+
- Response rate to reviews: 100% within 48 hours
