## Photo Strategy

Google gives higher ranking to profiles with regular fresh photos. Upload minimum 2 new photos per week during booking season.

**Photo categories to maintain:**
- At work (setup/delivery photos) — most important
- Exterior (unit at an event)
- Product (individual unit shots, clean background)
- Team (Harkirat at a setup — builds trust)
- Interior (inside the inflatables)

**Naming convention before upload**: `nova-kingdom-[unit-name]-[location]-[date].jpg`
Example: `nova-kingdom-crown-dino-combo-bridgewater-ns-june2026.jpg`
