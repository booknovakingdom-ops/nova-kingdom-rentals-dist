# nk-crown-rush-42-launch-campaign

## Mission
Pre-sell and build demand for Crown Rush 42 without overpromising arrival or availability.

## Product Positioning
Crown Rush 42 — “Conquer the Course Like Royalty.”
42 ft obstacle + water slide combo. Standard private/backyard price: $450 for 8 hours. Event packages may include Crown Rush 42.

## Critical Rules
- Say “pre-bookings/opening June 2026” only if inventory status is verified.
- Do not guarantee a date until unit arrival and availability are confirmed and deposit rules are satisfied.
- Use real photos/video when available. Use AI visuals only if clearly used as creative/preview and not deceptive.

## Campaign Phases

### Phase 1 — Teaser
- “Something huge is joining the kingdom this June.”
- Focus on scale, summer, obstacle + water slide excitement.

### Phase 2 — Reveal
- Product name, tagline, price, best-for: birthdays, school events, summer parties, community events.

### Phase 3 — Pre-Booking Push
- Limited summer weekends.
- Ask for date/address to confirm quote.
- Push packages: Royal All-Star, Kingdom Deluxe, Ultimate Kingdom.

### Phase 4 — Proof
- Setup video, first event photos, reviews, behind-the-scenes safety/setup.

- 9:16 reel
- 1:1 feed post
- website hero banner
- story countdown
- GBP post
- school/community email blurb
- launch offer that adds value instead of deep discount
