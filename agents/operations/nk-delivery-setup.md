## Site Assessment on Arrival

Before unloading:
```
[ ] Space check: Is the area large enough for the unit? (Check unit dimensions)
[ ] Surface: Flat grass preferred — flag any slopes >10 degrees
[ ] Overhead clearance: No low branches, power lines, or overhangs
[ ] Power access: Extension cord reaches a working outdoor outlet
[ ] Gate/access: CRV can get close enough for unloading
[ ] Sprinkler heads / hazards: Flag before staking

IF SPACE IS INADEQUATE:
→ Discuss alternative placement with customer before setup
→ If truly impossible: contact Harkirat immediately for resolution
```

## Setup Checklist (On-Site)

```
SETUP — Unit: ___  Customer: ___  Address: ___

[ ] Ground tarp laid flat — clear of debris
[ ] Unit unfolded and positioned correctly on tarp
[ ] Blower attached — intake clear
[ ] Unit inflating — watch for even inflation
[ ] Stakes / sandbags installed on all anchor points
[ ] Full inflation achieved — unit holds pressure
[ ] Entrance and slide clear and functional
[ ] Quick safety walkthrough — no sharp edges, no obstructions
[ ] Customer walkthrough:
    - Blower location pointed out
    - Weight/age limits communicated
    - Safety rules covered (no shoes, no food, one at a time on slide)
    - Harkirat's number given for any issues during event
    
[ ] SETUP COMPLETE — Time: ___
```

### Safety Rules to Cover with Every Customer:
1. No shoes inside the inflatable
2. Maximum [X] children at once (varies by unit — know your limits)
3. No food or drinks inside
4. Adult supervision required at all times
5. No flipping or rough play on the slide
6. If blower makes noise or unit deflates — call 902-990-0005 immediately

## Critical Rules
- **Always communicate an arrival window** — not a single time. Customers plan around this.
- **Site assessment before unloading** — never set up in an unsafe or impossible space.
- **Collect stakes every time** — they're easy to leave behind and safety-critical.
- **Photo the setup** when complete — timestamped proof of condition at delivery.
- **Photo the site** before teardown — protects against post-event damage claims.
- **Flag any issues in the teardown notes** → routes to Equipment Maintenance Tracker.
