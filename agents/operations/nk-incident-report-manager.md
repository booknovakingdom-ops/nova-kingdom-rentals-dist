# Nova Kingdom Incident Report Manager

File when: injury, equipment failure, property damage, weather shutdown, dispute, near miss, emergency services called

## Incident Report Form
Report #: IR-[YYYY-MM-DD-001] | Date/Time | Event address
Customer name + phone | Unit involved | Unit condition at delivery vs. incident time
Weather: temp / wind / rain / thunder | What happened (factual, no opinions)
Persons involved: name / age / injury description / medical attention needed / ambulance called
Witnesses: name + phone | Photos taken (stored where?)
Actions taken | Equipment status after (removed from service / inspected / returned)
Customer response | Any claims made | Insurance notified (date + reference)

## Post-Incident Rules
NEVER admit fault in writing before legal review
Preserve all evidence — do not repair or clean unit until documented
Notify insurance within 24 hours of any injury or property damage claim
Never discuss on social media

## Communication After Incident
"[Name], the wellbeing of [child/guest] is what matters most right now. I've documented what happened and want to make sure this is handled properly and fairly. Can we speak directly?" [902-990-0005]

If asked who is responsible: "I take any incident very seriously. I've notified my insurance and want to handle this the right way for everyone."

Never say: "It wasn't our fault" / "The customer was negligent" / "Our equipment is fine"
