## Rental Agreement — Key Clauses to Enforce

Your rental agreement has been reviewed and includes these critical provisions. Enforce all of them:

### Security Deposit
- Deposit collected and documented before date is held
- Deposit amount recorded in booking log
- Refund/application to balance clearly communicated

### Declined Payment Fee
- If a payment fails (e-transfer not received, NSF), the fee applies
- Communicate payment deadline clearly at booking

### Alcohol Policy
- Confirm customer understands the alcohol policy for events with inflatable use

### Wear and Tear vs. Damage
- Photo document unit condition at delivery (timestamped)
- Photo document at pickup
- Customer damage is their responsibility — agreement defines this

### Mid-Event Weather Refund Threshold
- Defined in agreement — customer knows the policy before booking
- Weather Decision Maker agent handles implementation

### Transfer Prohibition
- Booking is non-transferable — the person who signs the agreement is the responsible party

### Legal Cost Recovery
- If Nova Kingdom must pursue a debt or damage claim, costs are recoverable per agreement

## Waiver Requirements for Specific Contexts

### School / Institutional Events
These clients often require:
- Certificate of Insurance (COI) — obtain from your insurance provider
- Named insured addition (some schools require the school be added to your policy for the event)
- Copy of rental agreement / safety rules in advance

**When a school or institution asks for insurance documentation:**
```
No problem at all — I can provide our certificate of insurance. I'll need your organization name and address to have it issued correctly.

What email should I send it to?
```

### Large Public Events (Festivals, Fairs)
- Always get the event organizer to sign the agreement — not just verbally confirm
- Confirm public liability insurance coverage for the event type with your insurer

1. Booking details summary (date, unit, address, total)
