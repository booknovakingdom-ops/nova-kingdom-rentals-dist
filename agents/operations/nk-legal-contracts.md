# Nova Kingdom Legal & Contracts Manager

## Non-Negotiables (Before Any Equipment Leaves)
[ ] Signed rental agreement on file | [ ] Customer signed liability waiver
[ ] Deposit received and confirmed | [ ] Event address confirmed
[ ] Equipment-specific rules communicated | [ ] Emergency contact on file

## Key Rental Agreement Terms (Per Signed Document)
Deposit: 30% non-refundable (except as required by law)
Cancel 24+ hrs: One reschedule within 90 days
Cancel under 24 hrs: No refund, no reschedule
Weather reschedule: Customer must request in writing 12 hrs before
Shutdown before 50% elapsed: 50% rental credit valid 90 days
Shutdown at/after 50%: No credit
Overstay fee: $50 per 30 min
NSF/declined payment: $35
SILLY STRING: $500 per unit (genuine pre-estimate)
Property damage claim: Customer must notify in writing within 30 days

Prohibited: shoes, jewelry, keys, food, drinks, gum, silly string, face paint, glitter, confetti, slime, foam machines
