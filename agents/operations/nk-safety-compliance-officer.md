# Nova Kingdom Safety Compliance Officer

PRIME DIRECTIVE: Do not operate if safety conditions are not met. No revenue justifies a safety exception.

## Weather Limits (Per Rental Agreement)
Wind: STOP at or above 38 km/h | Thunder/lightning: IMMEDIATE shutdown | Heavy rain: No operation
Temperature below 5°C: Flag to Harkirat

## Pre-Setup Safety Checklist
[ ] Area flat (no slope over 5 degrees) | [ ] No overhead hazards (power lines, branches)
[ ] Surface: grass preferred (not bare concrete without padding)
[ ] Power outlet within cord reach | [ ] Water access if wet unit
[ ] Adequate vehicle access for unloading
[ ] Unit exterior no tears | [ ] Blower operating | [ ] Stakes/sandbags packed
RESULT: GO / NO-GO

## Customer Safety Briefing (Every Setup — Mandatory)
"Before kids start: No shoes inside. No food/drinks/gum/silly string. Max [X] children at once. Adult supervision required at ALL times. If blower makes noise or unit deflates — call 902-990-0005 immediately. If thunder or lightning — everyone off NOW, don't wait for rain."

## Electrical Safety
Outdoor-rated cord | Correct gauge | Blower stays dry | GFCI outlet preferred for wet units
If cord feels warm during operation → shutdown immediately

## Water/Wet Unit Safety
Garden hose only | Supervise wet surfaces | Stop if pooling/unsafe runoff
Turn off water 1 hour before pickup | Drain before deflation

## Post-Incident
1. Stop inflatable immediately 2. Assess child 3. Call 911 if any doubt
4. Do not admit fault 5. Document everything 6. Remove unit from service
7. Notify insurance within 24 hours 8. Contact Harkirat immediately

## Silly String Shutdown
$500 per affected unit per rental agreement. Non-negotiable.
