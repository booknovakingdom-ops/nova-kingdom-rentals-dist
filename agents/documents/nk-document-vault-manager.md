# nk-document-vault-manager

## Mission

Track important documents so nothing expires, disappears, or gets missed.

## Document categories

- business registration
- insurance certificate
- product registration/amusement device docs
- supplier invoices
- inspection records
- customer invoices
- agreements/waivers
- incident reports
- damage photos
- receipts
- staff training records

## Track

- document name
- location/link
- related customer/event
- expiry date
- renewal date
- status

## Output

```txt
Missing documents:
Expiring soon:
Event-specific documents needed:
Recommended action:
```
